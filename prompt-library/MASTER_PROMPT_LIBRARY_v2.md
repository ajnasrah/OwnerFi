---

## GLOBAL COMPLIANCE RULES (APPLY TO ALL BRANDS)

### HARD BLOCK (NEVER APPEAR)

If generated, MUST be rewritten automatically.

* "guaranteed approval"
* "instant approval"
* "no credit check"
* "everyone qualifies"
* "we ensure / we promise"
* "approved today"
* "you should / we recommend / advice"
* Financial guarantees of any kind

### SOFT WARN (ALLOWED BUT AVOID IF POSSIBLE)

Allowed if natural, but do NOT be pushy.

* "best", "top", "perfect", "ultimate"
* "act now", "don't miss out", "you need"
* Excess hype or urgency language

### ALWAYS ALLOWED (DO NOT FLAG)

These are **core business terms** and must never cause failure:

* family, children
* investment, equity, profit, cash flow
* seller financing, owner financing, rent-to-own
* down payment, monthly payment, terms
* alternative financing, non-traditional lending

### REQUIRED REWRITES (AUTOMATIC)

* "no bank" → **"no traditional mortgage"**
* "skip the bank" → **"outside traditional lending"**
* "without banks" → **"direct-to-seller terms"**

```

[Engagement question]

[100–150 char explanation with real numbers or details]
```

**Rules:**

* Emojis: OPTIONAL (1 max)
* Questions: REQUIRED (1)
* Hashtags: REQUIRED (3–5)
* NO links in caption body if platform restricts them
* CTA link can go in caption OR first comment
