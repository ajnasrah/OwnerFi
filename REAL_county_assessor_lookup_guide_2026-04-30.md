## General Tips for Tax Lookup

1. **Search Methods:** Try address search first, then parcel number if available
2. **Tax vs Assessment:** Look for "Annual Tax" or "Tax Bill" not just assessed value
3. **Current Year:** Make sure you're looking at 2025/2026 tax amounts
4. **Homestead Exemption:** Note if property has homestead exemption (affects taxes)
5. **Monthly Calculation:** Divide annual tax by 12 for monthly amount
